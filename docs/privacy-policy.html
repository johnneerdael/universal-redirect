Universal Redirector

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 7, 2025

This privacy policy outlines how the Universal Redirector Google Chrome extension ("the Extension") handles user data.

1. Information We Collect

The Extension collects and stores a minimal amount of non-personally identifiable information from users. This information includes:

  • User-Defined Redirect URLs: The Extension allows you to input and save custom URLs for redirecting specific websites. This data is stored locally in your browser's synchronized storage.

2. How We Use the Information

The information collected is used solely for the purpose of providing the core functionality of the Extension:

  • To apply the declarativeNetRequest API rules to redirect network requests from specified websites (e.g., Medium, YouTube) to the alternative front-end URLs you have defined.
  • To persist your custom redirection settings so they are available across your browser sessions.

3. Information Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All user-defined redirect URLs are stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.sync. This data is not transmitted to, or stored on, any remote server.
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We do not share, sell, or disclose any user data to third parties.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
